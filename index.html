Maintain Steady Speed
Driving at 60–80 km/h on highways improves fuel economy by up to 25% compared to aggressive driving.
🔧
Regular Servicing
A clogged air filter can reduce mileage by 10–15%. Timely oil & filter changes keep your engine efficient.
🌡️
Tyre Pressure
Under-inflated tyres increase rolling resistance. Check pressure every 2 weeks — correct pressure saves 3–4% fuel.
❄️
Smart AC Use
AC increases fuel consumption by 8–10%. Use at optimal settings (22–24°C) and turn off 2 minutes before stopping.
📦
Reduce Load
Every 50 kg of extra weight reduces fuel efficiency by ~2%. Remove unnecessary items from your boot.

📋 Comprehensive vs Third Party
Third Party: Legally mandatory. Covers damage to others only. ~₹2,000–4,000/yr.

Comprehensive: Covers your car + third party. Recommended for cars under 5 years. ~₹8,000–25,000/yr.
💡 IDV — Insured Declared Value
IDV is the maximum sum your insurer pays on total loss/theft. It equals the current market value of your car minus depreciation. Higher IDV = higher premium but better coverage.
🎁 No Claim Bonus (NCB)
For each claim-free year, you earn NCB discount (up to 50%!) on your next renewal. Never make small claims — it costs you the NCB which may be worth more.
⚙️ Useful Add-ons
Zero Depreciation: Full cost of parts without deduction — highly recommended.
Engine Protect: Covers engine damage from waterlogging.
RSA: Roadside assistance 24×7.
